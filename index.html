Welcome to my homepage. I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine. My research interests are in the areas of programming languages, systems, and security. I enjoy designing and developing scalable systems that can support static analysis on big code.

Currently, I am working in a project on Light-weight Capability Domains (LCDs) in the Linux Kernel at the MARS Systems Research Group with Professor Anton Burtsev.
